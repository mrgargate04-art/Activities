✨ The Skies Of The World ✨ A pastel sunset is a delicate, painterly masterpiece where the sky transforms into a soft canvas of muted color, often described as a "cotton candy" or romantic watercolor scene. Unlike a vibrant, fiery sunset, a pastel scene features gentle shifts from lavender to soft blush pink, pale coral, and dreamy lilac, often merging with a soft baby blue or turquoise sky.
